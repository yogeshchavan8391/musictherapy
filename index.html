Types of Music Therapy

Analytical Music Therapy

A therapist helps a client identify emotional themes in their music.

Benenzon Music Therapy

Combines psychoanalysis with music to help people find their "musical sound identity."

Cognitive Behavioral Music Therapy (CBMT)

Combines cognitive behavioral therapy (CBT) with music to reinforce or modify behaviors.

Nordoff-Robbins Music Therapy

Also called creative music therapy, this approach focuses on developing self-expression and communication.

Compositional Music Therapy

A patient works with a therapist to compose music to express their emotions.
